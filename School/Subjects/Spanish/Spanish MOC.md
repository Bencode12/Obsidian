## 📝 Key Grammar Topics

### Present Tense
- Regular verbs (-ar, -er, -ir)
- Irregular verbs (ser, estar, tener, ir)
- Reflexive verbs

### Past Tenses
- Preterite (completed actions)
- Imperfect (ongoing past actions)

### Vocabulary Themes
- Family and relationships
- School and daily routine
- Food and health
- Travel and culture
