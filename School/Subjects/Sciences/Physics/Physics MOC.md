## 📝 Key Formulas

| Topic | Formula | Variables |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Velocity | v = d/t | v=velocity, d=distance, t=time |
| Acceleration | a = Δv/t | a=acceleration, Δv=change in velocity |
| Force | F = ma | F=force, m=mass, a=acceleration |
| Kinetic Energy | KE = ½mv² | KE=kinetic energy, m=mass, v=velocity |
