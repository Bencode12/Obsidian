# 1.2 Change in Internal Energy by Work Done

## 📋 Learning Objectives

- [ ] Understand that internal energy can be changed by doing work
- [ ] Explain the relationship between work and heat
- [ ] Apply the first law of thermodynamics
- [ ] Solve problems involving work and internal energy change

---

## 🔬 Key Concepts

### First Law of Thermodynamics

**Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transferred or transformed.**

```
ΔU = Q - W
```

Where:
- **ΔU** = Change in internal energy (J)
- **Q** = Heat added to system (J)
- **W** = Work done BY the system (J)

### Sign Convention

| Process | Sign |
|---------|------|
| Heat added TO system | Q > 0 (positive) |
| Heat removed FROM system | Q < 0 (negative) |
| Work done BY system (expansion) | W > 0 (positive) |
| Work done ON system (compression) | W < 0 (negative) |

---

## 💡 Ways to Change Internal Energy

### 1. Heat Transfer
- Heating an object → Internal energy increases
- Cooling an object → Internal energy decreases

### 2. Work Done

**Compression (Work done ON gas):**
- Piston compresses gas
- Work done on the system (W < 0)
- Internal energy increases
- Temperature rises

**Expansion (Work done BY gas):**
- Gas pushes piston outward
- Work done by the system (W > 0)
- Internal energy decreases
- Temperature drops

---

## 🧪 Real-World Examples

### Example 1: Bicycle Pump
When you pump a bicycle tire:
- You do work compressing the air
- Internal energy of air increases
- Pump gets warm

### Example 2: Steam Engine
- Steam expands, pushing piston
- Steam does work on piston
- Internal energy of steam decreases
- Steam cools down

### Example 3: Refrigerator
- Compressor does work on refrigerant
- Refrigerant compressed (heats up)
- Heat removed in condenser
- Refrigerant expands (cools down)

---

## ✏️ Practice Problems

### Problem 1
A gas absorbs 500 J of heat and does 300 J of work. What is the change in internal energy?

**Solution:**
```
ΔU = Q - W
ΔU = 500 J - 300 J
ΔU = 200 J
```
**Answer:** Internal energy increases by 200 J

### Problem 2
A system has 800 J of work done on it and releases 300 J of heat. Find ΔU.

**Solution:**
```
Q = -300 J (heat released)
W = -800 J (work done ON system)
ΔU = Q - W
ΔU = -300 - (-800)
ΔU = 500 J
```
**Answer:** Internal energy increases by 500 J

---

## 📝 Key Equations

```
ΔU = Q - W
```

**Work done by gas (constant pressure):**
```
W = P × ΔV
```
Where:
- P = Pressure (Pa)
- ΔV = Change in volume (m³)
