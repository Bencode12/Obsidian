## What is Subtraction?

Subtraction means **taking away** or finding the **difference** between two quantities. Symbol: $-$

$$7 - 3 = 4$$

From seven objects, remove three — four remain.

---

## Subtraction on a Number Line

Start at the first number and **move left** by the second number.

$$9 - 5 \quad \longrightarrow \quad \text{start at } 9, \text{ move } 5 \text{ steps left, land on } 4$$

---

## Properties of Subtraction

### Not Commutative — order matters

$$a - b \neq b - a$$

$$7 - 3 = 4 \qquad \text{but} \qquad 3 - 7 = -4$$

### Subtracting Zero — no change

$$a - 0 = a$$

$$6 - 0 = 6$$

### Subtracting a Number from Itself — always zero

$$a - a = 0$$

$$8 - 8 = 0$$

---

## Relationship to Addition

Subtraction **undoes** addition — they are inverse operations.

$$5 + 2 = 7 \qquad \Longleftrightarrow \qquad 7 - 2 = 5$$

> [!tip] Use addition to check subtraction If $a - b = c$, then $c + b = a$. Always verify a subtraction result by adding back.
