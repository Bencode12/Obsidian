### **1.4.1 What is division?**

Division means **splitting into equal parts** or **repeated subtraction**.

Symbol: **÷** or **/**

Example:

- 12 ÷ 3 = 4  
    Twelve items split equally into three groups gives four per group.

### **1.4.2 Division as repeated subtraction**

- 12 ÷ 3  
    12 − 3 − 3 − 3 − 3 = 0  
    It took 4 subtractions → answer is 4

### **1.4.3 Relationship to multiplication**

Division undoes multiplication.

- 4 × 5 = 20
- 20 ÷ 5 = 4

### **1.4.4 Division is not commutative**

Order matters.

- 8 ÷ 4 ≠ 4 ÷ 8

### **1.4.5 Dividing by 1**

- 9 ÷ 1 = 9

### **1.4.6 Dividing zero**

- 0 ÷ 5 = 0

### **1.4.7 Division by zero (IMPORTANT)**

Division by zero is **undefined**.

- 5 ÷ 0 → not allowed

There is no number that satisfies this operation.
