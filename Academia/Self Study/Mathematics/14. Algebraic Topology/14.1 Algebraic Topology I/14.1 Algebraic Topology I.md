## Overview

Homotopy theory and fundamental group. Studies spaces up to continuous deformation.

---

## Core Topics

### [[14.1.1 Homotopy]]

Homotopy of maps, homotopy equivalence, contractible spaces.

### [[14.1.2 Fundamental group]]

Definition, computations for spheres, torus, projective spaces.

### [[14.1.3 Covering spaces]]

Lifting properties, classification via subgroups of π₁.

### [[14.1.4 Van Kampen theorem]]

Compute π₁ of unions from pieces.

### [[14.1.5 Higher homotopy groups]]

πₙ for n ≥ 2, differences from π₁.

---

## Key Definitions

**Homotopic Maps**: f ≃ g if continuous deformation exists.

**Fundamental Group**: π₁(X,x₀) = [loops at x₀].

**Covering Space**: p: E → X where each point has evenly covered neighborhood.

---

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Van Kampen**|π₁(X∪Y) from π₁(X), π₁(Y), π₁(X∩Y)|
|**Classification of Covers**|Covers ↔ subgroups of π₁|
|**Borsuk-Ulam**|Antipodal map Sⁿ → ℝⁿ has coincidence|

## Learning Objectives

- Define and compute fundamental groups
- Apply Van Kampen theorem
- Work with covering spaces
- Understand homotopy equivalence
- Distinguish π₁ from higher homotopy
