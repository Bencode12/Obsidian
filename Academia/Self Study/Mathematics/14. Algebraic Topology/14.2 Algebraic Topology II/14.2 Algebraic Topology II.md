## Key Definitions

**Chain Complex**: Sequence of groups with ∂² = 0.

**Homology**: Hₙ = ker(∂ₙ)/im(∂ₙ₊₁).

**Cohomology**: Hⁿ = dual of homology with cup product.

**Fiber Bundle**: Locally trivial projection p: E → B.

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
