## Overview

Advanced specialized topics building on foundations from analysis, algebra, and topology.

> [!info] Prerequisites
> Varies by topic—see individual sections

---

## Topics

### [[Differential Geometry]]

Manifolds, curvature, geodesics, Riemannian geometry.

### [[Lie Groups & Lie Algebras]]

Continuous symmetry, exponential map, representations.

### [[Ergodic Theory]]

Measure-preserving transformations, entropy, mixing.

### [[Operator Algebras]]

C*-algebras, von Neumann algebras, K-theory.

### [[PDEs]]

Classification, existence/uniqueness, weak solutions.

### [[Algebraic Geometry]]

Varieties, schemes, sheaves, cohomology.

### [[Category Theory]]

Categories, functors, natural transformations, adjunctions.

### [[Homological Algebra]]

Derived functors, Ext and Tor, spectral sequences.

### [[Spectral Theory]]

Spectrum of operators, functional calculus.

### [[6.5 Stochastic Calculus]]

Brownian motion, Itô integral, SDEs.

### [[Mathematical Logic & Set Theory]]

Completeness, incompleteness, forcing, large cardinals.

## Learning Path

Choose based on interests:

**Geometry/Physics Track:**
- Differential Geometry → Lie Groups → PDEs

**Algebra Track:**
- Category Theory → Homological Algebra → Algebraic Geometry

**Analysis Track:**
- Operator Algebras → Spectral Theory → Ergodic Theory

**Foundations Track:**
- Mathematical Logic & Set Theory

**Probability Track:**
- Stochastic Calculus
