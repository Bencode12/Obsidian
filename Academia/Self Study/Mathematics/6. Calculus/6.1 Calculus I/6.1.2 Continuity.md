### **What does continuity mean?**

A function is **continuous at a point** if its graph can be drawn **without lifting the pen** at that point.

### **Continuity on an interval**

- Continuous on (a,b)(a,b)(a,b) ‚Üí continuous at every point inside
- Continuous on [a,b][a,b][a,b] ‚Üí continuous inside + right/left continuity at endpoints

---

### **Continuity of common functions**

The following are continuous **wherever they are defined**:

- Polynomials
- Rational functions (except where denominator = 0)
- Exponential and logarithmic functions
- Trigonometric functions
