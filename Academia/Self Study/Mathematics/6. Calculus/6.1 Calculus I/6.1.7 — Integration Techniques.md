### **6.1.7.1 Integration by Substitution**

**Idea:**

- If an integral contains a function and its derivative, substitution simplifies it.

**Steps:**

1. Choose u=g(x)u = g(x)u=g(x) (inner function)
2. Compute du=g‚Ä≤(x)dxdu = g'(x) dxdu=g‚Ä≤(x)dx

5. Replace $u$ back with $x$

- Let u=x2‚áídu=2xdxu = x^2 \Rightarrow du = 2x dxu=x2‚áídu=2xdx
