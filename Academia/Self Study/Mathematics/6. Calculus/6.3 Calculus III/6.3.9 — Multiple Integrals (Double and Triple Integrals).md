## **7.1.9.1 Double Integrals**

For $f(x,y)$ over a region $R$ in the xy-plane:

∬Rf(x,y) dA\iint_R f(x,y) \, dA∬Rf(x,y)dA

- Represents **volume under the surface** z=f(x,y)z = f(x,y)z=f(x,y) over $R$
- dA=dx dydA = dx\,dydA=dxdy or dy dxdy\,dxdydx depending on order of integration

---

### **7.1.9.2 Iterated Integrals**

∬Rf(x,y) dA=∫ab∫g1(x)g2(x)f(x,y) dy dx\iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \int_a^b \int_{g_1(x)}^{g_2(x)} f(x,y) \, dy \, dx∬Rf(x,y)dA=∫ab∫g1(x)g2(x)f(x,y)dydx

- Integrate **inner variable first**, then outer
- Change order if necessary to simplify

**Example:**

∬Rxy dA,R:0≤x≤1, 0≤y≤x\iint_R xy \, dA, \quad R: 0 \le x \le 1, \, 0 \le y \le x∬RxydA,R:0≤x≤1,0≤y≤x ∫01∫0xxy dy dx=∫01x32dx=18\int_0^1 \int_0^x xy \, dy \, dx = \int_0^1 $\frac{x^3}{2}$ dx = $\frac{1}{8}$∫01∫0xxydydx=∫012x3dx=81

## **7.1.9.3 Triple Integrals**

For $f(x,y,z)$ over a 3D region $V$:

∭Vf(x,y,z) dV\iiint_V f(x,y,z) \, dV∭Vf(x,y,z)dV

- dV=dx dy dzdV = dx\,dy\,dzdV=dxdydz
- Represents **mass, charge, or volume under density**

**Example:**  
∭V1 dV\iiint_V 1 \, dV∭V1dV gives volume of $V$

## **7.1.9.4 Change of Variables (Polar, Cylindrical, Spherical)**

- **Polar coordinates:** x=rcos⁡θ,y=rsin⁡θ,dA=r dr dθx = r \cos\theta, y = r \sin\theta, dA = r\,dr\,d\thetax=rcosθ,y=rsinθ,dA=rdrdθ
- **Cylindrical:** x=rcos⁡θ,y=rsin⁡θ,z=z,dV=r dr dθ dzx = r\cos\theta, y = r\sin\theta, z = z, dV = r\,dr\,d\theta\,dzx=rcosθ,y=rsinθ,z=z,dV=rdrdθdz
- **Spherical:** x=ρsin⁡ϕcos⁡θ,y=ρsin⁡ϕsin⁡θ,z=ρcos⁡ϕ,dV=ρ2sin⁡ϕ dρ dϕ dθx = \rho \sin\phi \cos\theta, y = \rho \sin\phi \sin\theta, z = \rho \cos\phi, dV = \rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho\,d\phi\,d\thetax=ρsinϕcosθ,y=ρsinϕsinθ,z=ρcosϕ,dV=ρ2sinϕdρdϕdθ

## **7.1.9.5 Applications**

1. **Volume of a solid:**  
    ∭V1 dV\iiint_V 1 \, dV∭V1dV
2. **Mass with density:**  
    ∭Vρ(x,y,z) dV\iiint_V \rho(x,y,z) \, dV∭Vρ(x,y,z)dV

xˉ=1M∭xρ dV,yˉ=1M∭yρ dV,zˉ=1M∭zρ dV\bar{x} = $\frac{1}{M}$ \iiint x \rho \, dV, \quad \bar{y} = $\frac{1}{M}$ \iiint y \rho \, dV, \quad \bar{z} = $\frac{1}{M}$ \iiint z \rho \, dVxˉ=M1∭xρdV,yˉ=M1∭yρdV,zˉ=M1∭zρdV
