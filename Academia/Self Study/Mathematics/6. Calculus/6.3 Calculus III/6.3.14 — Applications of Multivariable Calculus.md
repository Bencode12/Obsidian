## **7.3.0.1 Optimization of Functions of Several Variables**

### **Second Derivative Test**

Let:

- D>0,fxx<0D > 0, f_{xx} < 0D>0,fxx<0 → **local maximum**
- D<0D < 0D<0 → **saddle point**
- D=0D = 0D=0 → test inconclusive
