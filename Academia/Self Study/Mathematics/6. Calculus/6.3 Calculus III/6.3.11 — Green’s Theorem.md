## **7.2.1.2 Interpretation**

- Converts **circulation around a curve** into a **sum of tiny rotations over the area**
- Useful for computing line integrals **without parameterizing the curve**

## **7.2.1.4 Conditions**

1. Curve $C$ must be **simple and closed**
2. Curve must be **positively oriented** (counter-clockwise around the region)
3. $P$ and $Q$ must have **continuous partial derivatives** on the region
