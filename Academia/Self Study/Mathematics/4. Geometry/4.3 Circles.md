### **4.3.1 Parts of a circle**

- **Circle**: set of all points equidistant from a center
- **Radius (r)**: distance from center to any point on the circle
- **Diameter (d)**: distance across the circle through the center → d = 2r
- **Circumference (C)**: distance around the circle → C=2πrC = 2\pi rC=2πr
- **Chord**: a line segment connecting two points on the circle
- **Arc**: part of the circumference
- **Sector**: region between two radii and an arc

### **4.3.2 Angles in circles**

- **Central angle**: vertex at the center → equals the arc it intercepts
- **Inscribed angle**: vertex on the circle → half of the intercepted arc
