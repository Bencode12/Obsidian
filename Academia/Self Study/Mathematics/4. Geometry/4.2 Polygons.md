### **4.2.1 What is a polygon?**

A **polygon** is a **closed figure** formed by **three or more straight line segments**.

- Each line segment is called a **side**
- The point where two sides meet is a **vertex**

Examples: triangle, quadrilateral, pentagon, hexagon

### **4.2.2 Types of polygons**

- **Triangle** → 3 sides
- **Quadrilateral** → 4 sides
- **Pentagon** → 5 sides
- **Hexagon** → 6 sides
- **Heptagon** → 7 sides
- **Octagon** → 8 sides
- **Nonagon** → 9 sides
- **Decagon** → 10 sides

### **4.2.3 Regular vs irregular polygons**

- **Regular polygon** → all sides and angles equal
- **Irregular polygon** → sides and/or angles are unequal

- Sum of **exterior angles** of any polygon = 360°
