## Almost Complex Structures

### Definition

**Almost complex structure** on smooth manifold $M$: Smooth tensor field $J \in \Gamma(\text{End}(TM))$ such that:

$$J^2 = -\text{id}$$

At each point, $J_p: T_pM \to T_pM$ with $J_p^2 = -I$.

Requires $\dim(M) = 2n$ (even dimension)!

### Examples

- **$\mathbb{C}^n$**: Standard complex structure
  $$J\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^k}\right) = \frac{\partial}{\partial y^k}, \quad J\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial y^k}\right) = -\frac{\partial}{\partial x^k}$$

- **Sphere $S^2$**: Complex structure from Riemann sphere
- **$S^6$**: Almost complex (from octonions), but NOT integrable!

## Complex Manifolds

### Definition

**Complex manifold** of complex dimension $n$: Manifold with atlas $\{(U_\alpha, \phi_\alpha)\}$ where:

- $\phi_\alpha: U_\alpha \to \mathbb{C}^n$ homeomorphism
- Transition maps **holomorphic**:
  $$\phi_\beta \circ \phi_\alpha^{-1}: \mathbb{C}^n \to \mathbb{C}^n \text{ is holomorphic}$$

Stronger than smooth manifold!

### Integrability

Almost complex structure $J$ is **integrable** if comes from complex manifold structure.

**Newlander-Nirenberg theorem**: $J$ integrable ⟺ Nijenhuis tensor vanishes:

$$N_J(X,Y) = [X,Y] + J[JX,Y] + J[X,JY] - [JX,JY] = 0$$

## Holomorphic Functions

### Definition

Function $f: M \to \mathbb{C}$ is **holomorphic** if in local coordinates $z^k = x^k + iy^k$:

$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial \bar{z}^k} = 0 \quad \text{for all } k$$

Cauchy-Riemann equations!

### Properties

- Holomorphic functions are smooth
- Identity theorem: Determined by values on set with accumulation point
- Maximum principle: No local maxima unless constant
- Liouville's theorem: Bounded entire function is constant

## Complex Tangent Space

### Complexification

Complexify tangent space:
$$T_pM \otimes \mathbb{C} = T_p^{1,0}M \oplus T_p^{0,1}M$$

where:
- $T_p^{1,0}M$: $+i$ eigenspace of $J$
- $T_p^{0,1}M$: $-i$ eigenspace of $J$

### Basis

In holomorphic coordinates $(z^1, \ldots, z^n)$:

$$T^{1,0}M = \text{span}\left\{\frac{\partial}{\partial z^1}, \ldots, \frac{\partial}{\partial z^n}\right\}$$

$$T^{0,1}M = \text{span}\left\{\frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{z}^1}, \ldots, \frac{\partial}{\partial \bar{z}^n}\right\}$$

where $\frac{\partial}{\partial z^k} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^k} - i\frac{\partial}{\partial y^k}\right)$.

## Dolbeault Operators

### Decomposition of Forms

Complex-valued $k$-forms decompose:
$$\Omega^k(M) \otimes \mathbb{C} = \bigoplus_{p+q=k} \Omega^{p,q}(M)$$

where $\Omega^{p,q}$ has $p$ $dz$'s and $q$ $d\bar{z}$'s.

### Dolbeault Operators

Exterior derivative splits:
$$d = \partial + \bar{\partial}$$

where:
- $\partial: \Omega^{p,q} \to \Omega^{p+1,q}$
- $\bar{\partial}: \Omega^{p,q} \to \Omega^{p,q+1}$

Properties: $\partial^2 = \bar{\partial}^2 = \partial\bar{\partial} + \bar{\partial}\partial = 0$.

---

## Dolbeault Cohomology

### Definition

**Dolbeault cohomology**:
$$H^{p,q}_{\bar{\partial}}(M) = \frac{\ker(\bar{\partial}: \Omega^{p,q} \to \Omega^{p,q+1})}{\text{im}(\bar{\partial}: \Omega^{p,q-1} \to \Omega^{p,q})}$$

Classes of $\bar{\partial}$-closed modulo $\bar{\partial}$-exact forms.

### Relation to de Rham

For compact Kähler manifolds:
$$H^k_{dR}(M) \otimes \mathbb{C} \cong \bigoplus_{p+q=k} H^{p,q}_{\bar{\partial}}(M)$$

Hodge decomposition!

## Examples of Complex Manifolds

### Complex Projective Space

$$\mathbb{CP}^n = (\mathbb{C}^{n+1} \setminus \{0\})/\mathbb{C}^*$$

Charts: $U_i = \{[z_0:\cdots:z_n] : z_i \neq 0\} \cong \mathbb{C}^n$

Compact complex manifold!

### Complex Tori

$$T = \mathbb{C}^n/\Lambda$$

where $\Lambda \subset \mathbb{C}^n$ is lattice of rank $2n$.

If admits Kähler metric: Abelian variety.

### Riemann Surfaces

Complex dimension 1 = real dimension 2.

Classification by genus $g$:
- $g=0$: Riemann sphere $\mathbb{CP}^1$
- $g=1$: Complex torus (elliptic curve)
- $g \geq 2$: Hyperbolic Riemann surfaces

## Holomorphic Vector Bundles

### Definition

**Holomorphic vector bundle**: Complex vector bundle with holomorphic transition functions.

Fibers are complex vector spaces, gluing maps holomorphic.

### Examples

- **Tangent bundle** $T^{1,0}M$: Holomorphic
- **Canonical bundle** $K_M = \Lambda^n T^{*1,0}M$: Holomorphic line bundle
- **Line bundles** $\mathcal{O}(k)$ on $\mathbb{CP}^n$

### Holomorphic Sections

Section $s$ is holomorphic if $\bar{\partial}s = 0$.

Much more rigid than smooth sections!

## Common Mistakes

> [!warning] Confusing Almost Complex and Complex
> Almost complex: Just $J^2=-1$. Complex: Also integrable (holomorphic charts).

> [!warning] Assuming All Even-Dimensional Manifolds Are Complex
> $S^4$ has no almost complex structure. $S^6$ has almost complex but not complex!

> [!warning] Forgetting That Holomorphic Is Stronger Than Smooth
> Many smooth constructions fail in holomorphic category (partitions of unity don't exist).
