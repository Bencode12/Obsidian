## Key Definitions

**Normed Space**: Vector space $X$ with norm $\|\cdot\|$ satisfying positivity, homogeneity, triangle inequality.

**Banach Space**: Complete normed space (every Cauchy sequence converges).

**Hilbert Space**: Complete inner product space (has geometry like Euclidean space).

**Bounded Operator**: Linear map $T$ with $\|Tx\| \leq C\|x\|$ for some constant $C$.

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Hahn-Banach**|Linear functionals can be extended while preserving norm|
|**Open Mapping**|Surjective bounded operators between Banach spaces are open|
|**Closed Graph**|Operator with closed graph is bounded|
|**Uniform Boundedness**|Pointwise bounded family is uniformly bounded|
|**Riesz Representation**|Dual of Hilbert space is isometrically isomorphic to itself|
