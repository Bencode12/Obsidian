## Key Definitions

**Basis**: Collection ℬ such that every open set is union of basis elements.

**Homeomorphism**: Bijective continuous map with continuous inverse.

**Hausdorff**: Distinct points have disjoint neighborhoods.

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Pasting Lemma**|Glue continuous functions on closed sets|
|**Tube Lemma**|Product of compact with any space behaves nicely|
