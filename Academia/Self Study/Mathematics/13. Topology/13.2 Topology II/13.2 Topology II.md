## Key Definitions

**Complete Metric Space**: Every Cauchy sequence converges.

**Quotient Map**: Surjective map where V open iff f⁻¹(V) open.

**Normal Space**: Disjoint closed sets have disjoint neighborhoods.

---

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Tychonoff**|Arbitrary product of compact spaces is compact|
|**Urysohn Lemma**|In normal spaces, separate closed sets by continuous function|
|**Tietze Extension**|Extend continuous functions from closed subsets|
|**Banach Fixed Point**|Contractions on complete spaces have unique fixed point|
