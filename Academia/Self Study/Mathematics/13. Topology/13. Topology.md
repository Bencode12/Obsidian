## Key Definitions

**Topological Space**: Set X with collection τ of open sets closed under arbitrary unions and finite intersections.

**Continuous Function**: f: X → Y is continuous if preimage of every open set is open.

**Compact**: Every open cover has finite subcover.

**Connected**: Cannot be written as union of two disjoint nonempty open sets.

---

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Heine-Borel**|Subsets of ℝⁿ are compact iff closed and bounded|
|**Tychonoff**|Arbitrary product of compact spaces is compact|
|**Urysohn Lemma**|Normal spaces have continuous functions separating closed sets|
|**Tietze Extension**|Continuous functions on closed subsets extend to whole space|
