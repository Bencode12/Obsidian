## **Lesson 9.1.6 — Determinants**

Determinants connect linear algebra to **geometry, invertibility, volume, and orientation**. They provide a scalar measure of a matrix’s “size” and its effect on space.

---

## **9.1.6.1 Definition of Determinant (2×2 and 3×3)**

### **2×2 matrix**

[  
A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \ c & d \end{bmatrix}  
]

[  
\det(A) = ad - bc  
]

### **3×3 matrix**

[  
A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \ d & e & f \ g & h & i \end{bmatrix}  
]

[  
\det(A) = a(ei - fh) - b(di - fg) + c(dh - eg)  
]

> Sign alternates; pattern generalizes to higher dimensions.

---

## **9.1.6.2 Properties of Determinants**

1. (\det(I_n) = 1)
    
2. (\det(A^T) = \det(A))
    
3. (\det(AB) = \det(A) \det(B))
    
4. (\det(cA) = c^n \det(A)) for (n \times n)
    
5. Swapping two rows flips the sign
    
6. Multiplying a row by (k) multiplies det by (k)
    
7. Adding a multiple of one row to another does **not** change det
    

---

## **9.1.6.3 Geometric Interpretation**

- (|\det(A)|) = **volume scaling factor** under linear transformation
    
- (\det(A) < 0) → orientation is reversed
    
- (\det(A) = 0) → space is “flattened” → **matrix is singular**
    

**Example:**  
(\det \begin{bmatrix}1 & 2 \ 3 & 6\end{bmatrix} = 0) → lines are parallel → no unique intersection.

---

## **9.1.6.4 Minors, Cofactors, and Laplace Expansion**

For (A = [a_{ij}]):

- **Minor** (M_{ij}) = determinant of matrix after removing row (i) and column (j)
    
- **Cofactor** (C_{ij} = (-1)^{i+j} M_{ij})
    

**Determinant via cofactor expansion:**  
[  
\det(A) = \sum_{j=1}^n a_{ij} C_{ij} \quad \text{(expand along row i)}  
]

---

## **9.1.6.5 Determinant and Invertibility**

[  
A \text{ invertible } \iff \det(A) \neq 0  
]

- Provides a quick test for solvability of (Ax = b)
    
- For invertible (A):  
    [  
    A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det(A)} \text{adj}(A)  
    ]
    

---

## **9.1.6.6 Determinant of Triangular and Block Matrices**

- Triangular: product of diagonal entries
    
- Block diagonal: product of determinants of blocks
