### **2.7.1 What is an equation?**

An equation is a **mathematical statement** where two expressions are equal.

Example:

x+5=12x + 5 = 12x+5=12

- The **unknown** (here xxx) is the value we want to find.

### **2.7.2 Solving simple equations**

To solve, isolate the unknown using **inverse operations**.

Example 1: x+5=12x + 5 = 12x+5=12

- Subtract 5 from both sides → x=12−5=7x = 12 − 5 = 7x=12−5=7

Example 2: 3x=153x = 153x=15

- Divide both sides by 3 → x=5x = 5x=5

### **2.7.3 Rules to remember**

1. Whatever you do to one side, do to the other
2. Combine like terms before solving
3. Check your solution by substituting back
