## What is an Equation?

An equation is a mathematical statement where **two expressions are equal**.

$$x + 5 = 12$$

The unknown ($x$) is the value we need to find.

---

## Solving Equations — Method

To solve, **isolate the unknown** by applying inverse operations to both sides.

|Operation in equation|Inverse operation to apply|
|---|---|
|$+ a$|$- a$|
|$- a$|$+ a$|
|$\times a$|$\div a$|
|$\div a$|$\times a$|

> [!tip] The golden rule Whatever you do to one side, you must do to the other.

---

## Worked Examples

**Example 1** — addition equation

$$x + 5 = 12$$ $$x = 12 - 5 = 7$$

**Example 2** — multiplication equation

$$3x = 15$$ $$x = 15 \div 3 = 5$$

**Example 3** — two-step equation

$$5x + 2 = 12$$ $$5x = 10$$ $$x = 2$$

**Example 4**

$$x - 7 = 10$$ $$x = 17$$

---

## Rules

1. Whatever you do to one side, do to the other
2. Combine like terms before isolating the unknown
3. **Always check** by substituting the answer back into the original equation

> [!example] Checking Example 3 $x = 2$ → $5(2) + 2 = 10 + 2 = 12$ ✅
