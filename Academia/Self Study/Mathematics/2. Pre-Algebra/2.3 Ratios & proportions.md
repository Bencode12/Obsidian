## Ratios and Proportions

Related: [[Fractions]] · [[Division]] · [[Percentages]]

---

## What is a Ratio?

A ratio **compares two quantities**, showing how many times one contains the other.

$$\text{Ratio} = a : b \quad \text{or} \quad \frac{a}{b}$$

> [!example] A basket has 8 apples and 4 oranges. $$\text{apples : oranges} = 8 : 4 = 2 : 1$$

---

## Simplifying Ratios

Divide both parts by their **greatest common factor (GCF)**.

$$12 : 18 \xrightarrow{\div 6} 2 : 3$$

---

## What is a Proportion?

A proportion states that **two ratios are equal**.

$$\frac{2}{3} = \frac{4}{6} \quad \Longleftrightarrow \quad 2 : 3 = 4 : 6$$

---

## Solving Proportion Problems

If $\dfrac{a}{b} = \dfrac{c}{d}$, then **cross multiply**:

$$a \times d = b \times c$$

> [!example] $$\frac{2}{5} = \frac{x}{15}$$ $$2 \times 15 = 5 \times x$$ $$30 = 5x$$ $$x = 6$$
