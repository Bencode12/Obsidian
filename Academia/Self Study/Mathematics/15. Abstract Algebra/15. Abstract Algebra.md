## Key Definitions

**Group**: Set with associative binary operation, identity, inverses.

**Ring**: Set with two operations (addition, multiplication).

**Field**: Commutative ring where every nonzero element has inverse.

**Homomorphism**: Structure-preserving map.

---

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Lagrange**|Order of subgroup divides order of group|
|**Sylow**|Existence of p-subgroups|
|**Fundamental Homomorphism**|G/ker(φ) ≅ im(φ)|
|**Galois Correspondence**|Fields ↔ subgroups|
