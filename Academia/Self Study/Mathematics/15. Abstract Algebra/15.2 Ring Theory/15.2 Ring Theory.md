## Key Definitions

**Ideal**: Subset closed under addition and absorption.

**Integral Domain**: Commutative ring with no zero divisors.

**Field**: Every nonzero element invertible.

**Algebraic Element**: Root of polynomial.

## Major Theorems

|Theorem|Statement|
|---|---|
|**Chinese Remainder**|Decompose rings via coprime ideals|
|**Hilbert Basis**|Polynomial rings over Noetherian rings Noetherian|
|**Fundamental Theorem of Galois**|Field extensions ↔ subgroups|
