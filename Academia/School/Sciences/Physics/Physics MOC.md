## 📝 Study Resources

### Problem-Solving Strategies
1. Identify known and unknown variables
2. Select appropriate equations
3. Check units and dimensions
4. Verify reasonableness of answer

### Common Formulas

**Thermodynamics**:
- $Q = mc\Delta T$ (heat transfer)
- $Q = mL$ (phase change)
- $\Delta U = Q - W$ (first law)

**Motion**:
- $v = s/t$ (velocity)
- $a = \Delta v / t$ (acceleration)
- $s = v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2$ (displacement)

**Forces**:
- $F = ma$ (Newton's second law)
- $F_g = mg$ (gravitational force)
- $P = F/A$ (pressure)
