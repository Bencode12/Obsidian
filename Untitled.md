- **Location:** Between the axon terminal of the sending cell and the dendrite of the receiving cell [[02:24](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OvVl8rOEncE&t=144)].
    
- **Signal Flow:** One-way, from the output portion (axon) to the input portion (dendrite) [[01:30](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OvVl8rOEncE&t=90)].
    
- **Signal Type:** Transitions from electrical (action potential) to chemical (neurotransmitters) and back to electrical [[03:26](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OvVl8rOEncE&t=206)].
    
- **Molecules:** Primarily neurotransmitters released into the synaptic cleft [[03:32](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OvVl8rOEncE&t=212)].
  
  
  |**Brain Parts**|**Place in the Brain**|**Function**|
|---|---|---|
|**Cerebrum**|The largest, uppermost part of the brain.|Responsible for **higher-order functions** like thinking, memory, emotions, and voluntary muscle movements.|
|**Cerebellum**|Located at the back of the brain, underneath the cerebrum.|Coordinates **balance, posture, and fine motor skills** (like typing or playing an instrument).|
|**Brainstem**|Located at the base of the brain, connecting to the spinal cord.|Controls **involuntary life-support functions** such as breathing, heart rate, and blood pressure.|
